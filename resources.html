Mathematics Digital Resources

Interactive Platforms

Engage students with dynamic platforms that transform abstract mathematical concepts into interactive experiences.

  • Khan Academy:

    Offers a wide variety of free video lectures and practice exercises on almost every topic in K-12 mathematics.

  • Purplemath:

    Provides free resources for learning algebra.

  • Illustrative Mathematics:

    Provides a complete set of free resources, curriculum, and professional learning for teachers to cultivate mathematical thinking in students.

  • IXL Learning:

    An immersive K-12 learning experience that provides comprehensive, standards-aligned content for math, language arts, science, and social studies.

  • Maths is Fun:

    This website provides clear explanations, diagrams, and interactive activities across various mathematics topics, from basic arithmetic to advanced topics. It's an excellent resource for both students and educators looking for a more visual and engaging approach to math.

  • FUSE - Department of Education & Training:

    FUSE is an initiative by the Department of Education & Training in Victoria, offering educational resources, digital learning materials, and more for teachers, students, and parents. The platform provides a wide array of content across various subjects and age groups.

  • NRICH:

    Provides a vast collection of math challenges, activities, and articles to deepen understanding. It caters to all age groups, making mathematics more accessible and engaging.

  • Project Euler:

    A series of challenging mathematical/computer programming problems that demand creative problem-solving and computational solutions. It's an excellent resource for those looking to combine math and coding skills.

  • Math Playground:

    Offers math games, logic puzzles, and a variety of problem-solving activities. Especially suitable for elementary and middle school students, it turns learning math into a fun and interactive experience.

Videos

Comprehensive video lectures that explain complex mathematical concepts in an easy-to-understand manner.

  • Numberphile:

    A YouTube channel that covers a wide variety of intriguing topics in mathematics. Created by Brady Haran, the channel features interviews with professional mathematicians discussing interesting numbers, problems, or theories.

  • 3Blue1Brown:

    Produced by Grant Sanderson, this YouTube channel offers intuitive visualizations of mathematical concepts, making complex ideas easily understandable. Topics range from linear algebra to calculus and beyond.

  • PatrickJMT:

    A YouTube channel offering a vast collection of math tutorials, from basic arithmetic to advanced calculus and linear algebra. PatrickJMT provides clear, step-by-step instructions for a multitude of mathematical problems, making complex topics more accessible.

Tools & Software

Enhance teaching and learning with specialized software tools tailored for mathematics.

  • Desmos:

    An advanced graphing calculator implemented as a web application and also as a mobile application.

  • Wolfram Alpha:

    A computational search engine great for solving mathematical problems and showing step-by-step solutions.

  • GeoGebra:

    Offers interactive mathematics software for learning and teaching math and science from primary school up to the university level.
